The old way — let's be honest
Liquidity Planning: nearly 3 years to MVP. Unified Worksheet: over a year for a single report. Cycle time is so long that every business case outside core TMS becomes impossible to defend. The roadmap calcifies. Customer Success can't promise anything new. Innovation dies in the backlog.
That's the MBA tower. Three layers of foundation and no marshmallow.
The new way
One person. AI coding agents. Prototype in weeks. Test with real data. Iterate daily. Kill what doesn't work before it costs anything.
That's the kindergartner.
What changes
The cost of experimentation drops to near zero. Features that took years become prototypes in weeks. Business cases that couldn't survive a 3-year cycle become viable. Customer Success gets something to show. The roadmap opens up — because we can finally afford to try things that aren't guaranteed wins.
